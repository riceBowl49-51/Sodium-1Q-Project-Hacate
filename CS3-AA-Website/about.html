ABOUT

Heyyy, I’m RICE, I go to school at a school on the surface of the Earth (yep, super specific, I know). I made this site because I wanted to dig into the world of fast fashion— how it looks cool on the outside but has some messy secrets hiding underneath.

I’m not here to guilt-trip anyone, just to share what I’ve learned and spark a little curiosity about how our clothes affect people and the planet. Fashion can still be fun, creative, and stylish without making a huge mess. If you’re down for that, then we’re already on the same page.

I chose this topic because fast fashion is something we all interact with almost daily, yet most people don’t realize how much it impacts both the environment and the workers behind the clothes. Learning about it made me want to spread awareness and show that style doesn’t have to come at such a high cost.

– RICE, served hot with a side of hot fits ;>
